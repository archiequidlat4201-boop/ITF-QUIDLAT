How to Play

  1. Parachute onto the island and choose your landing spot.
  2. Search buildings for weapons, armor, and supplies.
  3. Stay inside the safe zone as it shrinks.
  4. Use vehicles to move quickly across the map.
  5. Eliminate enemies and be the last survivor!
